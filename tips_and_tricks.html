Multiplication Tips

1. For 9 × any number, the digits of the answer always add up to 9!
Example: 9 × 7 = 63, and 6 + 3 = 9

2. To multiply by 4, double the number twice!
Example: 15 × 4 = (15 × 2) × 2 = 30 × 2 = 60
